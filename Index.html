About Us

📖 Our Mission

Dawat-e-Islami Online Academy is a global Islamic educational platform founded by Hafiz Muhammad AFAQ Attari. Our mission is to provide authentic Quranic education with Tajweed, Tafseer, and Islamic Masail to Muslims all around the world.

🎓 Who We Are

We are a team of Ijazah-certified scholars and qualified teachers dedicated to spreading the light of Quran and Sunnah. Our academy offers one-to-one live classes, flexible timings, and personalized attention for each student.

🌍 Global Reach

Students from any country can join our academy. We have successfully taught students from UK, USA, Canada, Australia, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Switzerland, South Africa, India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore, and many more.

✅ What We Offer

• Madani Qaida for beginners
• Quran recitation with proper Tajweed
• Quran translation (Tarjuma) word-by-word
• Detailed Tafseer of Quran
• Islamic Masail according to Hanafi Fiqh
• Hifz (Memorization) program
• Arabic language course
• Special program for children and sisters
