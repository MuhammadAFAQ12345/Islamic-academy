📖 Surah Al-Baqarah (Ayat 1-3)

🌙 Intro

Surah Al-Baqarah Quran-e-Majeed ki sab se azeem aur lambi surah hai...

الٓمّٓۚ (1) ذٰلِكَ الْكِتٰبُ لَا رَیْبَ فِیْهِ ۚۛ-هُدًى لِّلْمُتَّقِیْنَۙ(2)

الَّذِیْنَ یُؤْمِنُوْنَ بِالْغَیْبِ وَ یُقِیْمُوْنَ الصَّلٰوةَ وَ مِمَّا رَزَقْنٰهُمْ یُنْفِقُوْنَۙ (3)

🌟 Tafseer

Yeh Quran ki pehli ayat insaan ko hidayat aur imaan ka paigham deti hai...

“La rayba feehi” ka matlab hai ke is kitab mein koi shak nahi...

💡 Key Points

  • Quran hidayat ki kitab hai
  • Imaan sirf belief nahi, amal bhi hai
  • Namaz aur sadqa musalman ki pehchan hai

🤲 Conclusion

Jo insaan Quran ko samajh kar apni zindagi mein amal kare, wohi asal kamyab hai.

📌 Yeh ek choti si koshish hai Quran ko asaan banane ki.
